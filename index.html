Biography

Yongcheng Liu is currently a 4th-year Ph.D. candidate in the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition-NLPR, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences-CASIA, supervised by Bin Fan and Chunhong Pan. Before his Ph.D. pursuing, he received a B.E. degree of Control Technology and Instrument in Huazhong University of Science and Technology-HUST.

Yongcheng Liu has wide research interests in Computer Vision and Remote Sensing, with focus on 3D Point Cloud Processing, Image Segmentation, Object Detection and Multi-Label Image Recognition driven by Deep Learning.
